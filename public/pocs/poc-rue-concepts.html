Rue — Mascot Direction Review

4 distinct character directions for Rue, the Pyrch owl AI. Each explores a different personality axis. Review at the top of each card, then see how Rue looks in real UI contexts before deciding.

Brand brief: warm, wise, family-friendly Palette anchor: amber eyes #F59E0B Must work at 16px → 512px
OPTION A

Friendly Rue

Round. Warm. Immediately loveable.

The Duolingo play. Large round head, massive amber eyes, soft ear tufts, gentle color palette. Works for every age — kids adore it, parents trust it. This is the safe choice that could also become iconic with consistency.

OPTION B

OPTION C

Icon Rue

Bold. Graphic. Unmistakable at 16px.

Forget feathers — this is pure geometry. Navy circle, cream face, two giant amber eyes. It's the owl as a logo mark. Works on app store icons, favicons, embossed on merchandise, printed at 1cm. The boldness makes it memorable. This is the Apple SF Symbol approach: clarity over decoration.

🍭 ROUND 2 — TOOTSIE POP INSPIRED

Tootsie Pop Direction

Three new directions inspired by Mr. Owl from the classic Tootsie Pop commercial. The iconic round glasses are the defining feature. Same navy/amber/cream palette, three different personality layers.

OPTION E

The Professor

Purest Tootsie Pop — no extras, just the classics

The most faithful interpretation: big round glasses with thick navy frames, amber lenses, and a wide trapezoidal beak. Clean, immediately recognizable, and authoritative without being intimidating.

🦉 ROUND 3 — DUOLINGO INSPIRED

Duolingo Direction

Three options applying the three things that make Duolingo’s owl work: white sclera behind the iris (gives eyes life), thick arched eyebrows above the eyes (gives expression), and a small cute beak (friendly, not authoritative). Same Pyrch navy/amber/cream palette throughout.

OPTION H

Duo Core

Content expression — the everyday Rue

The three things that make Duolingo's owl work, applied to Rue: white sclera behind the amber iris, thick arched eyebrows above the eyes (not above glasses), and a small cute beak. The white sclera is the missing ingredient from all previous options.

Strengths

  • ✓White sclera makes the eyes pop — instantly more alive than all prior options
  • ✓Thick brows give expression without needing a mouth
  • ✓Small beak reads as friendly, not authoritative
  • ✓Scales cleanly — the simpler shape holds at 20px

Tradeoffs

  • △Most similar to Duolingo — need to ensure it feels like Rue not Duo
  • △Neutral expression may need brow variants for celebration states
OPTION I

Duo Bright

Curious/excited expression — Rue when she finds something

Same structure as H but with the brows arched higher, iris slightly larger, and pupils rounder. The raised brows change the read from "content" to "curious" — like Rue just spotted something interesting about your household.

OPTION J

Duo Warm

Duolingo structure + Option D warmth — the recommended pick

Takes the Duolingo structure (white sclera, arched brows, small beak) and layers in the warmth signals from Option D: cheek blush and violet body accent. The white sclera gives it the life the earlier warm options lacked. This is where the Duolingo direction and Pyrch brand intersect.

Round 3 recommendation

  • •  Cleanest / most scalable → Option H (Duo Core)
  • •  Most expressive / dynamic → Option I (Duo Bright)
  • •  Most brand-distinctive + warmest → Option J (Duo Warm) ⭐ recommended
  • •  Overall top pick across all rounds → Option J if you love the Duolingo feel, or Option D if you prefer no glasses at all. Both have the cheek blush. Both have the violet.
