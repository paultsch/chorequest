Review Notes

After reviewing each POC, consider the following for your Pyrch decision:

  • Which nav pattern feels right? (sidebar / top bar / bottom tabs)
  • Where should Rue live? (sidebar, chat panel, inline card, floating button)
  • Should the chore health view be on the home page or a separate route?
  • Dark or light as the default theme?
  • Should child profile cards be the hero, or should the approval queue be primary?
  • Is the AI-split layout (POC 4) too complex for v1, or is it the right north star?
